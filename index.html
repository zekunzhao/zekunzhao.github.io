Bio

Zekun Zhao is a Ph.D. student working with Professor Jeffrey Flanigan in the Computer Science and Engineering department at UC Santa Cruz. His research focuses on large language model inference with knowledge-based reasoning. He obtained his B.Eng. at Nankai University in Intelligent Science and Technology. He worked at JMing-Ming Cheng's Lab and Feng Duan's Lab.

Research Interests

Zekun's research interests in developing methods that enhance both the understanding and generation of natural language with an emphasis on efficient and effective performance.
  • Natural Language Processing (natural language grounding, knowledge-based reasoning, inference speedup)
  • Verification in AI (formal verification for generative AI, neural theorem proving)
  • Machine Learning (deep learning, self-supervised learning, reinforcement learning, multimodal machine learning)
