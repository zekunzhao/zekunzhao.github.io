Bio

Zekun Zhao is a master student of Computer Science and Engineering at UC Santa Cruz. He obtained his B.Eng. at Nankai University in Intelligent Science and Technology. Currently he is working with Professor Jeffrey Flanigan. He worked at JMing-Ming Cheng's Lab and Feng Duan's Lab.

Research Interests

Zekun's research interests include Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning, with an emphasis on building embodied AI agents that can communicate with humans using natural language to perform real-world tasks.
  • Natural Language Processing (natural language grounding, knowledge-based reasoning, multilingual understanding)
  • Computer Vision (vision and language, visual navigation and robotics, activity understanding)
  • Machine Learning (deep learning, self-supervised learning, reinforcement learning, multimodal machine learning)
